Cookie Cleaner Settings

These settings apply whenever you clear cookies from the popup, the keyboard shortcut, or auto-clean.

Include subdomains by default

Also remove cookies set on subdomains of the active site (e.g. mail.example.com when on example.com).

Clear local storage & IndexedDB

Also wipe localStorage, IndexedDB, and service workers for the site, not just cookies.

Clear cached files

Also clear the browser cache scoped to the site's origin.

Auto-clean on tab close

When the last tab for a site is closed, automatically clear its cookies in the background.

Show notifications

Show a system notification after a keyboard-shortcut or auto-clean run.
